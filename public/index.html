Our Ministries

Ten pillars of service, all rooted in the love of Christ

🙏

Spiritual & Evangelical

Preaching the Gospel, planting churches, and conducting revival programs locally and internationally.

📚

Education & Training

Bible schools, discipleship programs, scholarships for orphans and underprivileged children.

🏘

Social Development

Food, shelter, housing construction, and care centers for the elderly and vulnerable.

🏥

Women & Youth

Skill development, entrepreneurship, and leadership for women and youth empowerment.

🌍

Disaster relief, rehabilitation, and emergency support for affected communities.

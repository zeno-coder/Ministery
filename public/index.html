Our Mission

  • To plant Bible training schools globally and train people to spread the Gospel worldwide.
  • To establish schools across Africa.
  • To empower women through skill training and income-generating initiatives.
  • To provide counselling and spiritual guidance for children, teenagers, and youth.
  • To raise leaders who impact nations spiritually and socially.

Our Vision

  • To equip and edify believers for the expansion of the Kingdom of God.
  • To present every believer mature in Christ (Colossians 1:28).
  • To build apostolic training centers in nations.
  • To establish Christian schools across Africa.
  • To build an orphanage for vulnerable children.
  • To build care homes for the sick, including cancer patients.
  • To see communities transformed through education, empowerment, and discipleship.
